## Cross-cutting themes

1. **Coarse-PK silent-loss is a recurring class** (F-1324). 0053–0056 fixed 4 tables; ≥6 siblings remain. Needs a systematic "every per-source event table PK carries an event-instance discriminator" sweep + a lint (the lint-pk-discriminators script exists — extend its allowlist enforcement).
2. **Config `default:`-tag vs `Default()` drift is systemic** (F-1327) and underlies the most dangerous latent bug (F-1316). One reflective drift-test closes the whole class.
3. **Observability has a large dead layer** (F-1329): 18/106 alerts can't fire, incl. backups and page-severity DB/ZFS alerts. The metric-name lint checks names but not rule-expression resolvability — extend it.
4. **Doc drift is the single largest finding category** — binding architecture docs, ADRs, runbooks, CLAUDE.md, and the deployed OpenAPI all lag ~6 weeks of change (ClickHouse pivot, projector, retention removal, SemVer, single-host reality). Invariant-8 (retention) drift-seeds appear in ≥12 places.
5. **Test rot** (F-1334): the suites that would catch regressions (k6 SLA proof, integration, ops integration) are themselves broken and unrun in CI.
6. **Shutdown/durability** (F-1317, F-1318, F-1350): the indexer's crash/SIGTERM paths lose non-recoverable served-tier data; no shutdown-sequencing tests exist.
